A few books to read

January 22, 2019     books reading  

An Article titled Top Learnings from 43 books I’ve read in 2018 caught my attention as I was going through my Medium daily digest; I have been trying to get more into reading given that it has never been one of my primary interests, except when I read stuff related to my profession.

Reading wasn’t exactly an activity fostered in my family and I personally never took the time to dive in until now; like much other activities, having a frame of reference and recommendations from active and eager readers is a great influence and starting point.

So here is the list from the article I should be getting sometime soon:

  • The Four: The Hidden DNA of Amazon, Apple, Facebook and Google
  • Tribe of mentors
  • Blink: The power of thinking without thinking

This should be enough for my ambitious personal goal for a few months, besides, I am trying also to squeeze in literature. I may update this same page as I find more of my interest.
